    Share a Shopping List

    You can use this app for your own lists for free. Whenever you update your list all devices that share the list get updated. So if you notice you are out of tofu you can pop open your smartphone and add it to your list. Someone from your houshold who may be at the store would automatically see tofu on their list. You can checkout Peri's and my list here.

    This is an HTML5 mobile app which means that it will run on iPhones, Androids, windowsPhones, ipads, tablets, laptops...anything with a browser. Once you have a list setup you can add it to you home screen and it will work just like any app.
